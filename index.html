Who We Are

An organisation of data science enthusiasts, technologists and consultants passionate about using technology to create a social impact.

Established in 2018, we at IIT Kanpur Consulting Group are driven by the vision of using data science and case study solving skills to help organizations leverage better insights from their data, and thereby create a strong social impact. Following are the domains we work in:

Environment

Environmental challenges are the primary issues our planet is facing at the moment. We at ICG use various public data to build effective monitoring and predictive models that help the government to identify key sectors to tap in and optimize environmental feasibility of projects.

Healthcare

With growing amount of data available in the healthcare sector, we aim to develop tools and models that accurately predict onset of epidemics and diseases. This domain is full of new possibilities and ICG works for making the world more aware and more accessable in terms of healthcare.

Business

Our organization offer consulting services to companies, primarily startups that cannot avail services of premier consulting firms. Our model is pro-bono in this context, and our services range from business development strategies to market entry research and much more.

Education

We believe the key to a holistic development of the world begins at better education. We incorporate and build solutions that directly impact the education sector, right from the beginning. In this process, we also extend a hand to those companies, who wish to transform the scenario using technical advancements.

How we work

We recruit the best talent and use the latest techniques of data science and case solving.

For every project, we recruit students from our campus, with specific focus on both students with domain knowledge in the project as well as students who wish to learn data science and case solving by hands-on experience. Each project is mentored by alumni of our group who currently work at leading consulting firms such as The Boston Consulting Group, McKinsey and Bain.

Talent

The talent we recruit for projects includes people of all levels of expertise, right from a fresher interested in data science to final year students who would join respected consulting companies in the following year.

Data science

Our group believes in the saying that goes around these days that data is the most valuable oil. Members working in projects include those who have internship experience in data science in big corporates as well as those who have successfully won data science hackathons.

Case solving

We leverage alumni experience as well as internship experience in case solving to deliver the best solutions to social problems.

Our work

These are the projects we have worked on.

Completed Projects

Machine Learning based Air Quality Forecasting in Delhi-NCR

We did a project with Prof. SN Tripathi of the Department of Civil Engineering, IIT Kanpur in association with the National Clean Air Programme (NCAP), Ministry of Environment, Government of India in which we predicted levels of pollutants such as PM2.5 and PM10 over time and space in Delhi-NCR. This was being done using machine learning techniques of time series forecasting and Long Short Term Memory (LSTM) and incorporating weather, traffic and other data from different sources into the model. The model is meant to help the government in devising necessary policy interventions to protect the health of citizens against the detriments of bad air quality.

National Clean Air Program Program launched by the Government of India to prevent, control and reduce air pollution
Improving the GFS weather forecasts for mitigating farmer risks in agriculture

Our work revolved around using machine learning to improve granular weather forecasts (temperature, rainfall and humidity) to help them with minimising the farmer's risk and efficient crop management.

Weather Risk Management Services Pvt. Ltd. Smart farming company that was founded in 2004 with a vision to empower farmers to overcome the most daunting agrarian challenges
Lead Scoring and Course Recommendation Strategy

Our task was to reduce customer acquisition costs by prioritising the marketing efforts towards candidates with a high Lead scoring probability. Keeping this in mind, we had built a machine-learning-based customer priority model to prioritise marketing efforts towards customers with the highest probability of conversion. This wase followed by a course recommendation strategy for curated selling of online courses to target customers (using natural language processing).

Undisclosed Ed-tech start-up to teach working professionals the latest skills and courses for advancing their career
Business Model for a Social Ecommerce Platform for Service Industries in India

We worked on market size estimation, customer acquisition strategy as well as 5-year revenue model for their mobile application. Our work was used by the client in their pitch deck for securing seed funding for their start-up, as well as for their customer acquisition strategy in Bangalore.

Recko Social commerce platform co-founded by ex-McKinsey and ex-BCG consultants
Mitigating the Risk of Recurrence of Polio Disease in India

This project was carried out with help from Rotary India to identify regions in India which are at a high risk of recurrence of polio disease. This was followed by a resource allocation model to help Rotary with its mitigation efforts towards combating polio recurrence in India. The project was presented at Summer Research Seminar, The University of Tokyo and the research manuscript is under review for publication in relevant conferences.

Rotary India Social organization

Ongoing Projects

Predicting the evolution of deforestation in a spatial zone

We are doing a project partnered with Moja Global and Makerere University with the funding from Google AI in which we predict the evolution of deforestation in a spatial zone. This is being done using machine learning techniques of time series forecasting and Long Short Term Memory (LSTM) over satellite images and incorporating ground reports collected by Makerere University. This model would help the stakeholders in making better informed policy choices around climate change.

Google AI Every year Google selects 6 proposals from all over India that aim to leverage artificial intelligence and machine learning in order to solve pressing global challenges like poverty, infant mortality, deforestation etc.
Anomaly detection and protocol recommendation system in absence of a qualified doctor

We are doing a project partnered with Care India with the funding from Google AI. Care India is wrestling with infant mortality in Bihar, where mothers do not have access to proper healthcare and doctors are in very short supply. We are developing an AI-enabled anomaly detection and protocol recommendation system for midwives and nurses to guide them in the absence of a qualified doctor.
